Objective

This page offer service as home, psychological assistance and legal advice for women who are living a case of domestic violence in Colombia.

Strategy

Women who are living a case of domestic violence can found a home to live in different places in the country to live with their children, also they can program meetings with psychologist and lawyers to solve their situation.

The houses belong to women who are volunteer and want to fight against domestic violence in Colombia. They can register their house in the page.
